# Changelog

## 0.2.0 (2026-02-12)

### Quest Overhaul

- Add quest chapters "Overview", "Twilight Forest", and "Cataclysm"
  - "Overview" shows major milestones of the modpack. Quests here are hidden until all parent quests have been completed
  - "Twilight Forest" shows Twilight Forest progression. Related quests from "The Ascent" and "The Summit" were moved here
  - "Cataclysm" shows Cataclysm progression. Related quests from "The Pinnacle" were moved here
- Remove quest chapters "Introduction", "Affix Loot", "Helpful Mods", and "The Pinnacle"
  - Some quests from "The Pinnacle" were moved to "The Summit"
- Rename "The Beginning", "The Ascent", "The Summit" to "Early Game", "Mid Game", "Late Game"
- Chapters other than "Overview" are revealed only when players reach that stage of progression
- Hide quest text until quests are unlocked

Overall, these changes aim to make the quest book less overwhelming and more structured.

### Mod Changes

- Remove Overflowing Bars
- Add Dynamic RPG Resource Bars
  - Health bar, hunger bar, etc. are only displayed when not full
  - Armor bar was removed because there is (practically) no maximum armor
- Bump mod versions to the newest versions as of 2026-02-12

### Other Additions

- Add tooltips for Chests, Quest Book, Torch, and Spell Books
  - They replace quests in "Helpful Mods" for serving just-in-time hints
